The Property

Where Every Window
Frames the Water

Welcome to 331 Land Street — a stunning waterfront retreat offering the ultimate island lifestyle. Just 2 years old, every detail of this 1,500 sq ft home has been thoughtfully crafted for luxury living, seamless entertaining, and effortless rental income potential.

The home features 3 spacious bedrooms, each with its own private ensuite bath, top-of-the-line LVP flooring throughout, and a chef's kitchen with a GE Cafe appliance package and walk-in pantry. Designer tile showers, tile hearths, and custom backsplashes add a polished finish to every room, while canal and bay views from every window ensure you are never far from the water.

Step outside to screened porches on both the front and back, an extra-large fenced backyard with a fire pit, and a beautifully sodded Zoysia lawn with oyster shell landscaping. The front porch delivers breathtaking sunset views, and dolphin and manatee visits are a regular occurrence right from your property.

With nearly all furniture and interior decor available for a turnkey transition, this property is equally suited as a primary residence or a high-performing short-term rental. Everything is like new — this is island living at its finest.
